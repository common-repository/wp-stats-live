== Description ==

WP Stats Live provides real time data for your blog. you Don't need to refresh your page as this updates in real time. You can see who is online, who is a real person, who is a bot, where people came from, when they last clicked, if they are still on your blog, what their IP is, Choose who provides IP information, Information is also in the header of your blog admin so there is no need to refresh any pages.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin to your blog's plugin directory (plugin folder).
	usually, public_html/wp-content/plugins/

2. Login to your wp-admin dashboard & proceed to your plugins management page. Activate the WP Stats Live plugin


3. Go to settings and you will find the tab "WP Stats Live" and thats it!
